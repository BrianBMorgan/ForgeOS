Event Overview

Welcome to the Intel Federal Summit 2025. This guide contains everything you need to know before attending the event, including venue information, agenda highlights, logistics, and key contacts.

What to Expect

  • Keynote presentations from Intel leadership
  • Technical deep-dive sessions
  • Hands-on demonstrations
  • Networking opportunities
  • Partner showcase exhibitions

Who Should Attend

  • Federal IT decision makers
  • Government technology leaders
  • Defense and intelligence professionals
  • Systems integrators
  • Channel partners

Venue Information

The Intel Federal Summit will be held at a premier conference facility in the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area, easily accessible from major airports and transit lines.

Venue Details

Location: Washington, D.C. Metropolitan Area

Parking: Complimentary parking available for all attendees

Public Transit: Metro accessible with shuttle service from nearby stations

Getting There

By Air

  • Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (DCA) — closest option
  • Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD)
  • Baltimore/Washington International Airport (BWI)

Ground Transportation

  • Rideshare services available
  • Hotel shuttle services
  • Rental car facilities at all airports
  • Metro rail connections
